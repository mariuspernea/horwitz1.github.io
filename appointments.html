APPOINTMENTS

Private

Private appointments can be arranged through Mr Horwitz's Practice Manager, Karen on 0208 150 8290. Or you can book directly with the consulting venue of your choice.

Most private health insurance companies require you to be referred by your GP or another specialist. Please bring the referral letter with you to your first appointment. Self-pay patients do not require a formal referral.

If you have any queries about this process, please contact Karen on 020 8150 8290 or use the contact form. Private appointments, and any subsequent treatments, take place at the locations listed here Mr Horwitz operates privately on adults at the Platinum Medical Centre, Wellington Hospital and on children at the Harley Street Clinic Children's hospital.

NHS

Mr Horwitz is a full time Consultant in the Hand unit at Chelsea and Westminster Hospital.

All NHS appointments require a referral from your GP, specialist or Accident & Emergency Dept. For details about appointments, please visit The Chelsea & Westminster Hospital Appointments Page.
